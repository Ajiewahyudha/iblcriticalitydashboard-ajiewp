Methodology

Standards & Assessment Logic

The criticality determination is anchored in recognised international reliability standards. Below is the exact logic applied to every material line.

Risk Priority Number

IEC 60812 — Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA/FMECA)
RPN = S × O × D

Severity (S), Occurrence (O) and Detection (D) are each rated on a 1–10 scale. Severity reflects the consequence on the parent equipment and plant; Occurrence is derived from the actual unscheduled-breakdown / maintenance-order reservation rate; Detection reflects how readily degradation is revealed by current controls. A higher RPN signals higher risk priority.

HIGH
S ≥ 9 OR RPN ≥ 200
MEDIUM
RPN ≥ 100
LOW
otherwise
